Jonathon Sims

Jonathon Sims is the Head Archivist of The Magnus Institute, London.

He took over the position in 2016 after previous Head Archivist, Gertrude Robinson, went missing

Jon enjoys letting out his suppressed inner theater kid by dramatically reinacting horrific statements while recording them in the privacy of his office. (He thinks that the rest of the archival crew don't notice but come on Jon, we've all listened to the tapes).

Despite his passion for his work, Jon does not believe any of the statements given to the institute. Definitely not. Nope. Total skeptic... Absolutely...

On a completely unrelated note, Jon does not like spiders, and will jump about a foot in the air if you say the words "knock knock" within a thirty mile radius of him.

Factoid "Average human being has 3.5 eyes" is a statistical error! The average person has two eyes! Jonathon Sims, who has a completely unreasonable number of eyes, is an outlier, and should not have been counted!

Jon keeps a cot on site. Sometimes we wonder if he even goes home.
