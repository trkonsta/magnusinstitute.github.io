The Magnus Archives is a weekly horror fiction podcast examining what lurks in the archives of the Magnus Institute, and organisation dedicated to researching the exotic and the weird. Join Jonathon Sims as he explores the arvhive, but be warned, as he looks into its depths something starts to look back...

The Magnus Archives is a podcast distributed by Rusty Quill and licensed under a creative commons attribution non-commercial sharealike 4.0 international license. It is written and performed by Jonathon Sims and directed by Alexander J. Newall.
